# USENIX Annual Technical Conference 2026

## About USENIX ATC 2026

The USENIX Annual Technical Conference (USENIX ATC) is the premier venue for presenting and discussing advanced systems research. USENIX ATC 2026 will bring together leading researchers, practitioners, and industry experts to share the latest developments in computer systems, including operating systems, distributed systems, cloud computing, storage, networking, and security.

## Conference Information

- **Dates**: July 15-17, 2026
- **Location**: Boston, MA, USA
- **Venue**: Boston Convention and Exhibition Center
- **Format**: Hybrid (In-person and Virtual)

## Call for Papers

USENIX ATC 2026 seeks original research papers that advance the state of the art in systems research. We welcome papers on all aspects of computer systems, including but not limited to:

### Topics of Interest

- Operating systems and runtime systems
- Distributed systems and cloud computing
- Storage systems and file systems
- Networking and network protocols
- Security and privacy in systems
- Virtualization and containerization
- Machine learning systems
- Edge computing and IoT systems
- Performance analysis and optimization
- Reliability and fault tolerance
- Energy efficiency and sustainability
- Systems for emerging hardware

## Submission Guidelines

### Paper Format

- Papers must be submitted in PDF format
- Maximum length: 12 pages (excluding references and appendices)
- Use the USENIX LaTeX template (available at [usenix.org](https://www.usenix.org))
- Papers must be anonymized for double-blind review
- Submissions must be original work not published or under review elsewhere

### Evaluation Criteria

Papers will be evaluated based on:
- Technical merit and innovation
- Clarity of presentation
- Relevance to the systems community
- Reproducibility and experimental methodology
- Potential impact on the field
